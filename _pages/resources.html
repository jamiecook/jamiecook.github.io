Face Recognition

There are some great Face Recognition resources available on the internet, I find a good place to start is face-rec.org, especially their comprehensive coverage of the many face databases that are available.

Recently I also found some additional Annotations of selected databases provided by Tim Cootes for use with Active Appearance Models (AAM).

  • Manual annotation of the BioID face images.
  • Manual annotation of the XM2VTS face images.
  • Manual annotation of the AR Face Database face images.
  • Images with annotation of a talking face.
