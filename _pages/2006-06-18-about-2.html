My name is James Cook, but I prefer to be called Jamie so as to avoid the obvious comparisons that arise when you share a name with the person who is credited with discovering Australia. That is me over there on the left, I'm a software developer / manager / research engineer from Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. I currently work for Veitch Lister Consulting, managing their R&D team to help deliver transport modelling software.

Previously I completed my Elec. Engineering PhD in the field of “3D Face Recognition” at QUT where I also did my Bachelor of I.T. and Bachelor of Electronic Engineering. I haven't worked in that discipline since moving to the field of traffic and transport forecasting but if (for some strange reason) you are interested in my work, a list of my publications is available for your perusal.

I also have two beautiful daughters, but because this site is for my technical interests I'll save all those photos for my google+ stream.
