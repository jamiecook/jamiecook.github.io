If you haven't been following the story, the $100 laptop is an initiative cooked up by millionaire philanthropist Nicholas Negroponte to provide cheap laptops to kids in Africa. That is a very condensed version of the story, but it is the essence - check out the official site for more information.

The rhetoric and opinions have been flying over this program ever since it was first announced but now I am happy to say that there is a physical product which we can all "ooohhh and aahhh" over - MIT has produced the first prototypes of the $100 laptop and pictures are available at (and it makes me very happy to say it) flickr.
