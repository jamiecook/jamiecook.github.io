A while back I saw an article describing how scientists had connected a flight simulator to a petri dish of rat brain cells and that those cells were doing a passable job of keeping the flight simulator running on a smooth course. Well it appears that these scientists from Japan have gone one step further with this approach and have build a robot which is controlled by a light sensitive type of slime mould. Thats right this little six legged robot will scuttle away from bright lights and find itself a dark humid corner to hide it.... weird but true.
