Poor form on keeping a regular post I know but ..... well I have no
excuse really so I'm just going to tell you all about the latest
adventures I've been having.

Yesterday my buddy Chris and I trekked along the muddy banks of the
delaware river looking for a rope swing that a friend had recommended
and given (shitty as it turns out) directions to. So we almost turn
back after having walked about two miles but decided to press on and
were rewarded with the most beautiful little slice of river life. This
rope swing had to be secured 30 meters up into a huge tree and we were
swinging from about 10 off the water... meaning that when we let go we
were pretty close to 10 meters above water level. Appartently my face
was pretty funny as I was falling and I suddenly realised that "hey
shouldn't I have hit water by now???"

I also managed to hurt myself pretty bad.. I have an incredible rope
burn on my bicep which I shall endeavour to post photos of (yes it is
that bad that it warrants online photos). I also think I sprained my
ring finger (how I did that or why I bothered to write this escape me
now). Anyways, adventures, good fun... I think we'll go do it again
today.

Peace out
