I just saw a couple of articles on a company called Vidient, who are in the process of rolling out their SmartCatch technology to the international airport in Helsinki. They claim to offer the ability to "automatically monitors CCTV footage and can recognise
potential security threats. It also flags up operational issues such as
building queues and bottlenecks of human traffic through customs and
security."

They have also been behind government funded program to incorporate the same smart surveillance technology in San Francisco International. Asked about whether this system had ever actually stopped any known terrorists airport security manager Paul Foster replied "Did I stop a bad guy or did I just stop an employee from doing something they shouldn't be doing? I can't be certain. People in security look at things differently." Well dodged!
