Two quotes today (or perhaps motto is more appropriate) which were commonly found on public clocks in previous years:

  • ultima forsan ("perhaps the last" [hour])
  • vulnerant omnes, ultima necat ("they all wound, and the last kills").

and one which I actually found as a the title from a Streets song

  • Memento Mori ("Remember your death") - It is a statement and an artform apparently

A bit morbid but there you go.
