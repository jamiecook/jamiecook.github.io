Saw this little article on wired and thought I'd preserve it for when I do some more web-development. Basically it's a method for growing thumbnails into the fullsized image directly on the page - full description or just a demo for those of you who don't care about how it's done.

From the demo I could think of a couple of enhancements, namely there should only be one image full sized at a time, if you click on a second it should minimize the first one. And why does it even need to be a click - mouseover should work just as well.
