Hey everyone, that is if anyone still reads this now, prolly not but at
least I'm still reading. I had my 25th birthday on Friday and despite
thinking that it would be a bit of a dud affair I managed to get a jam
packed weekend happening, thanks to the 4th of July celebrations which
spread it out to 3 days of fun.

Friday

Work finished up nice and early and I went out with a couple of
my workmates to get a late afternoon climb in. Hadn't been for a while
but still managed to knock out a couple of good climbs, also makes for
a nice natural high before I went out in Manhattan for a decidedly
chemical high. My flatmate got a bit of a crew together and we cruised
over for dinner and drinks in a couple of sweet bars around his little
brothers apartment on the east side.

Saturday

Woke up feeling pretty hot in a small NY apartment and made it feel a
bit better with a bunch of good coffee the next morning and some great
eggs benedict. Pity about Davenport losing the Wimbledon finals but
can't have everything.

Once I got back to princeton after some shopping, I had dinner and went
out to a cowboy type bar and stayed out until a bit too late.

Sunday

Did nothing except cleaning up my apartment, grocery shopping and washing clothes.  Nothing doing !  Sweet!

Monday

Monday started out slow, but it was 4th July so I had to do something.
So I got a call from my man Chris with a wacky tubing idea, we ended up
sitting in the hot sun on an inner tube floating down the delaware
river at a very leisurely pace.

Then went back to his place for a bbq with his family which was a nice
little slice of American Pie. Then two of his friends, Megan and BJ,
came over and picked us up for a trip into Philli. We got to see some
awesome acts for free on the steps of the Art Museum, Bryan Adams
(stoked... not), Rufus Wainright and the real reason that I went .....
Elton John! I know I could get some shit from people about that but I
couldn't pass up the opportunity to see someone influential and
inspirational to so many people. And he was excellent! truely
sensational.

Anyways, it's now Tuesday night and I've hardly slept so I shall leave you with that little peek into my birthday weekend.

Ciao
Jamie
