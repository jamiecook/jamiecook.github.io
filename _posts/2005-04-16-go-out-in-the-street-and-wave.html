I'm sure that at least some of you heard that silly internet rumour
going around a little while ago (around 1st of April if memory serves).
Google had just announced their new satellite imagery acquisition and
some people would have you believe that if you stood out side your
house looking like a goose and waving at the sky that you'd come out as
a blurry blob on their maps.

I gave it a miss but apparently they occasionally capture some really interesting things during their automated sweeps.
