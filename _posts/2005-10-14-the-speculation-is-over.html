Thats right people the wait is over, you no longer have to dedicate brain power to figuring out who the next James Bond will be. And if you have laid money on it you are either going to be very, very happy or simply left scratching your head. Without further ado the new James Bond is ...... 
Daniel Craig!!!!

Who? I hear you ask, I have personally only seen one of his movies "Layer Cake" (which is from the Lock Stock series) and while he did a decent job of playing the lead he didn't really bring a whole lot of personality to it. Something which I'm sure most people will agree with me in as being a requisite attribute for 007.

Well as I don't know too much about him, I'll wish him luck and wait to see how long he lasts.
